About us

Who We Are

The organization was founded on the belief that every individual has the right to quality education and that they should have access to this through the provision of the right resources in order to realize their dreams.

Deeksha Educational Trust was founded in 2019 with the mission of providing this quality education in order to revolutionize the way young individuals train and enhance their skills to become successful professionals.

Our Mission

“To empower persons with quality education, enabling them to live their life with dignity and respect.” Everyone has the right to know all their options before making a decision. Our mission is to provide information regarding all these choices.

Our Vision

Nurturing Today’s Young People, Inspiring Tomorrow’s Leaders

Our vision is wide-ranging. It encompasses aspiration, educational excellence, system leadership, social mobility and cohesion.
